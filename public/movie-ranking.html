Movies Worth Watching in 21st-century

The top three Movies/or Series

Game of Thrones

A medieval fantasy epic based on George R.R. Martin’s book series “A Song of Ice and Fire.”
Two powerful families vie for control of the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros.
Notable for its complex characters, political intrigue, and epic battles.

The See

Set in a post-apocalyptic future where humanity has lost its sense of sight.
Twins born with the ability to see become central to the plot.

365 Days

A Polish erotic thriller about a mafia boss who gives a woman 365 days to fall in love with him.
Widely panned as a repugnant, offensive, and laughable ripoff of “Fifty Shades of Grey.”
